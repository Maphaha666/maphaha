About Me

I am a 19-year-old BSc in Mathematics and Applied Mathematics student at UNISA with distinctions in Computer Science, Physics, and Advanced Mathematics. I'm a passionate tech enthusiast who thrives in independent learning and problem-solving environments.

I specialize in front-end development using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, and I've built interactive projects like a chess game, typing speed tester, and math quiz app. I've also participated in coding bootcamps such as WeThinkCode_, gaining real-world coding experience and teamwork exposure.

Through platforms like Coursera and Udemy, I continuously expand my skills in Python, Java, C++, and web development. When not coding, I mentor fellow students and explore how mathematics and technology can solve real-world problems.
